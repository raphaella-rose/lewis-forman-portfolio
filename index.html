LEWIS FORMAN

Creative Producer

Hi, I'm Lewis!

I'm a Creative Producer based in Edinburgh, UK.

I am a recent graduate from the CGO Institute Diploma in Creative Producing, and will soon be graduating from The University of Edinburgh with an MA in Linguistics.

I started producing 7 years ago, and you can check out some of my more recent work here.

In 2019, I launched The Counterminers, an Edinburgh based comedy theatre collective, with the aim of devising, writing and creating new work in a collaborative fashion. It is through the Counterminers, as well as through several theatre societies at The University of Edinburgh that most of my producing experience comes from.
